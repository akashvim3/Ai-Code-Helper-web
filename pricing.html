Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the cost accordingly.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your access will continue until the end of your billing period, and you won't be charged again.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! Students and educators get 50% off on all paid plans. Contact us with your educational email for verification.

Is my code data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and never store your code permanently. We're SOC 2 certified and GDPR compliant.
