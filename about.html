Our Journey

Our Story

CodeAssist AI was founded in 2023 by a team of developers who were frustrated with the limitations of existing coding tools. We spent countless hours researching, prototyping, and testing to create an AI assistant that truly understands developers' needs.

What started as a small side project has grown into a platform trusted by over 500,000 developers worldwide. Today, we're backed by leading investors and continue to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI-powered development tools.
